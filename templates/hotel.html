Conference Venue Information

Hyatt Regency, Gurgaon, Delhi NCR

ILMUNC India {{ g.ILMUNC_year }} will be held at the Hyatt Regency, Gurgaon, Delhi NCR. This five-star hotel has been specifically chosen to host ILMUNC India as it is well suited to accommodate a high-quality MUN experience. It is located just 19 kilometres from Connaught Place, 17 kilometres from Chandni Chowk market and a 40 minute drive from the Indira Gandhi International Airport, making it easily accessible by transport.

The hotel, featuring 451 guest rooms, also offers luxurious recreational facilities, an outdoor swimming pool, and the finest dining options. Delegates and faculty advisors will also be able to enjoy free Wi-Fi on an unlimited number of devices in guest rooms and social spaces.

Exquisitely designed rooms feature elegant furniture and floor to ceiling windows with a view overlooking the landscaped garden or swimming pool. Guestrooms offer a plush king bed, work desk with high-speed Internet access, evening turndown services and complimentary use of the fitness center and swimming pool.
